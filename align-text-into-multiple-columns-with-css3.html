Investment income, money earned by financial assets or financial accounts, comes in three basic forms: interest, dividends, and capital gains. Bonds generate interest; stocks generate dividends; and capital gains (profits) can come from any investment. Accruals are earned revenues and incurred expenses that have yet to be received or paid. Accounts payable are short-term debts, representing goods or services a company has received but not yet paid for. The Total liabilities attributable to unit holders.
